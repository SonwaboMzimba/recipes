Ingredients: (for 2)
 - 4 cups samp
 - 3 cups sugar beans
 - 2 chopped onions
 - 4 chopped tomatoes
 - salt
 - Aromat
 - 1kg of mince

Directions:
 1. boil samp and beans in one pot for aproximately 4 hours and season with salt
 
 2. fry the onions, tomatoes, and mince together and season with aromat
 
 3. once samp and beans are ready, mix with mince together.
